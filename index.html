Origami Designs and Facts

Step by Step diagrams are probably the most popular and easiest to follow way to show how to fold things out of paper. Trying to find good origami instructions on the Internet can be a lot of work though. To help your search we’ve put together the largest database of free origami diagrams anywhere on the Internet.

Apple


  • The science of apple growing is called pomology.
  • 25 percent of an apple’s volume is air; that’s why they float.
  • Apples are a member of the rose family of plants, along with pears, peaches, plums and cherries.

Dove

  • Doves are able to adapt and survive in just about any climate.
  • Doves are some of the strongest fliers of any bird species.
  • The term “dove” is rooted from a Germanic word that refers to their tendency to dive.

Fish

  • There are over 30000 known species of fish.
  • They have been on Earth for more than 500 million years!
  • Fish make up about half of all known vertebrate species.

Swan

  • They are very large birds and can weigh up to 30 pounds
  • Swans are highly intelligent and sharp vision and impeccable hearing.
  • Swans live for approximately 20 to 30 years.

Turkey

  • Turkeys are intelligent and sensitive animals that are highly social.
  • Turkeys have the ability to learn the precise details of an area over 1,000 acres in size.
  • Turkeys have 5000 to 6000 feathers.

Puppy

  • Puppies are born blind and deaf
  • They like when you sing-talk to them
  • Puppies know how to manipulate you with their eyes
